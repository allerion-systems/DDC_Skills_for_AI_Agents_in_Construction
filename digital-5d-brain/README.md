# Digital 5D Brain

**Built for James “Joey” Allee and ALLERION Systems.**

The Digital 5D Brain is a personal operating system for turning a life of projects, documents, ideas, estimates, photos, videos, code, and conversations into an AI-searchable knowledge graph.

This repo is the first public foundation for Joey’s personal brand, project portfolio, low-dollar Digital Brain offer, and future ALLERION product ecosystem.

## Core promise

> Build your brain once. Use it forever.

## The 5 dimensions

1. **Identity** — who Joey is, what he believes, and what he is building.
2. **Projects** — every project, company, job, build, and experiment he has touched.
3. **Proof** — artifacts, estimates, proposals, screenshots, photos, drawings, notes, commits, and lessons learned.
4. **Products** — free and paid offers that turn the system into revenue.
5. **Agents** — AI teammates that operate over the Digital Brain.

## Build rule

Every artifact should become one of three things:

1. **A memory** — what happened.
2. **A proof object** — evidence that it happened.
3. **A product asset** — something that can teach, sell, automate, or compound.
